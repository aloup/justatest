Here at Paws for a Cuppa, we bring in adoptable cats from the Ottawa Humane Society to meet potential adopters. All kinds of kitties, from the cutest kitten to the most lovable senior cat, have found new homes through the cat cafe.

Feel free to make an appointment to come in and view our cats up for adoption. You can interact with them and see if they'll be a good fit for you and your home!

These cats average at about three years old, but there have been months-old kittens and older cats, as well. Regardless of age, we make sure to find them the perfect forever home.

The cats are usually mixed breed, regular domestic cats. We have the occasional pedigree, but mostly try and focus on those that are less likely to be taken up by a larger adoption agency. Our utmost goal is to help our furry friends!

Everyone who has adopted a cat from Paws for a Cuppa gained a new forever friend! We have the most successful adoptions out of all the cat cafes in our area.

Thank you for visiting! We look forward to your appointment to come to our cafe and consider adopting one of our sweet cats!
