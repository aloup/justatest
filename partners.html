Our Partners


What it sounds like when the cats greet us in the morning!

Paws for a Cuppa works with local cat rescues in order to promote adoptions of cats and kittens. Although our main cast of cats are permanently adopted at the cafe, from time to time we will foster adoptable cats. Being at the cafe helps young cats and kittens to learn how to interact with humans, and provides them with much-needed exercise! If you find yourself bonding with one of our adoptable cats, please ask the staff for information about adopting.

Paws for a Cuppa also runs regular educational and awareness events, where experts from our partner institutions answer cat-related questions and help squash commonly held myths about cat ownership. Check out our calendar for the next upcoming event!
