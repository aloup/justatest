About Us

  • Cat Cafes originated in Taiwan in 1998, and quickly became popular, not only to the locals, but also to the various tourists from other countries, most importantly Japan. The idea was taken back to Japan, and the first cat cafe implemented in Japan sprung up in 2004.

  • Visiting a cat cafe helped people relieve stress by kicking back and relaxing with a furry friend. They proved an excellent distraction from the monotony and difficulty of urban life, and therefore were very popular with tourists and locals alike. They can now be found in:
    • Korea
    • Austria
    • Spain
    • Germany
    • France
    • The United Kingdom
    • Canada
    • The United States



  • Here in North America, the rules are slightly different than our counterparts in Japan, where everything is kept very clean. We separate the cats and the food, and the two cannot be mixed. But please still feel free to give the cats a visit after enjoying your snack or beverage outside of the playpen!
